Drawing and Modeling Standards for Revit

The purpose and intent of this document is to provide guidance for unifying modeling and drawing with the Revit platform. The intent is not to regulate or enforce process or procedure but rather to demonstrate what the end result should be. Throughout the document you will come across advice on best practices. The advice given is based on the current state of Revit and experience with the current state of prototypical models. With that in mind, what may be a best practice today may not be in a week or month. Your help in keeping advice and suggestions up to date is encouraged and appreciated.
